01 — Objective

Why we ran this audit

Review the website's conversion points and key pages to enhance parent conversions — optimising these pages for better performance and evaluating how they can be structured and organised.

Analysis focus
1 · First Impressions & Messaging

Does the site immediately communicate the school's value and engage prospective parents?

2 · User Journey & Navigation

How easily can parents find information and progress towards an enquiry or application?

3 · Content & Trust

Does content build confidence through clear messaging, social proof and compelling reasons to choose the school?

4 · Conversion Experience

How effective are the CTAs, forms and the overall enquiry and enrolment journey?

Key pages reviewed

The conversion points that matter most for prospective families

  • Homepage & enrolment journey
  • Junior School (K–6)
  • Middle School (7–9)
  • Senior College (10–12)
  • Events / Open Day registration
