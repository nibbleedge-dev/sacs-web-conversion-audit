01 - Objective

Why we ran this audit

Review the website's conversion points and key pages to enhance parent conversions - optimising these pages for better performance and evaluating how they can be structured and organised.

Analysis focus:

1. First Impressions & Messaging: Evaluates whether the website immediately communicates the school's value and engages prospective parents.

2. User Journey & Navigation: Assesses how easily parents can find information and progress towards an enquiry or application.

3. Content & Trust: Reviews whether the content builds confidence through clear messaging, social proof and compelling reasons to choose the school.

4. Conversion Experience: Examines the effectiveness of CTAs, forms and the overall enquiry and enrolment journey.

Key Pages:

The conversion points that matter most for prospective families

  • Homepage & enrolment journey
  • Junior School (K–6)
  • Middle School (7–9)
  • Senior College (10–12)
  • Events / Open Day registration
